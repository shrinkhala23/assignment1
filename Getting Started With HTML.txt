Ans.4- TAGS:- Tags are the starting and ending part of the HTML elements.They begin with < symbol and end with > symbol are called tags.
Example- <h1> </h1>
ELEMENTS:-  Elements enclose the content in between the tags. They consist of some kind of structure or expression. 
Example- <h1> this is the content</h1>
